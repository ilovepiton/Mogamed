# Klims Office — Plan

## What is Klims Office?

Klims Office is my personal automatic dashboard.

Every morning it should show:

- important AI news from Telegram channels
- pictures from Telegram posts if they exist
- 10 Catalan words for the day
- quick links
- daily mission

---

## Main pages

### 1. Home

The main dashboard.

It shows:

- 3–5 most important AI news
- Daily Catalan words
- Quick Links
- Daily Mission

---

### 2. AI News

A separate news page.

Rules:

- read posts from connected Telegram channels
- use picture only if the original Telegram post has a picture
- if there is no picture, show the news without a picture
- remove trash, ads and duplicates
- rate importance from 1 to 5
- show only important news on Home

---

### 3. Catalan

Daily language learning page.

Rules:

- 7 new words every day
- 3 old words for review
- translation
- example sentence
- small quiz later

---

### 4. Links

Beautiful buttons for useful websites.

Categories:

- AI
- Study
- Projects
- Tools
- Fun

---

### 5. Settings

Settings page.

It will contain:

- Telegram channels
- update time
- language settings
- news importance rules
